Reporting a Regulations Error

The STR regulations table is one of the most valuable (and most time-sensitive) parts of HostCalc. If you find something wrong — an outdated permit requirement, a missing city-level restriction, an incorrect tax rate — please email me with:

  • The state (and city/county if applicable)
  • What's currently shown vs. what's correct
  • A source link if you have one (government website preferred)

I take these seriously and update the table when I can verify the correction. STR rules change faster than any single publisher can track — community corrections genuinely help.

What I Don't Do

HostCalc doesn't provide financial, tax, legal, or investment advice. I can't tell you whether you should buy a property, how to structure your STR for tax purposes, or whether your local ordinance allows your listing. For that you need a licensed professional who knows your specific situation.

I also can't help with individual Airbnb account issues — for those you'll need to contact Airbnb's host support directly.
