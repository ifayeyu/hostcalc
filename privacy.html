Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Overview

HostCalc ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at hostcalc.co. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Short version: Your calculation data never leaves your browser. All numbers you enter into HostCalc stay local and are gone when you close the page. We only collect standard analytics and advertising data — described in full below.

Information We Do Not Collect

Every calculator on HostCalc runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your nightly rate, occupancy, revenue, expense figures, and any other inputs are never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not store any calculation or financial data.
  • We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address.

Information We Do Collect

Like most websites, we automatically collect some basic technical information when you visit:

  • Log data — your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This is collected automatically by our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages).
  • Analytics data — we may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (pages viewed, session duration, geographic region). This data is anonymized and not personally identifiable.
  • Cookies — see the Cookies & Advertising section below.

Cookies & Advertising

HostCalc is supported by display advertising. We use Google AdSense to show ads on this site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit How Google uses data from sites that use our services.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — advertising: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — analytics: Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Pages — hosting: Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google's servers: Google Privacy Policy

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or California, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Because we collect minimal personal data, there is very little to act on — but you can contact us at the email below with any requests.

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out or Your Online Choices (EU).

Children's Privacy

HostCalc is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at: hello@hostcalc.co
